Resolved in Fragmentation


Curated by Ayobami Adegbite


This exhibition examines the paradox of artworks that are both unfinished and complete, exploring how incompleteness becomes an integral part of their final form. Joan Heemskerk and Dirk Paesmans, as Jodi, created fragmented, glitchy interfaces in the mid-1990s that disrupt user expectations, turning digital errors into a finished artistic experience. Pete Gomes' Loop (1998) is a collaborative web project that embraced perpetual evolution, where each participant’s contribution added to a continually unfinished yet cohesive whole. Déclin Séquence exemplifies this theme with its 31 individually styled <time> elements, representing each day of October 2020, where the interplay between daily updates and the slider’s browsing options blurs the line between process and completion. These works challenge traditional notions of artistic finality.
